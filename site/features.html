Features & Amenities

A rustic, yet comfortable cabin with full modern conveniences.

  • Master Bedroom: Sealy Posturepedic king-size bed, lake view.
  • Second Bedroom: Comfortable guest bedroom with bedding.
  • Living Area: Cozy great room with wood-burning stove, TV and home-theater setup.
  • Handcrafted Cedar Kitchen: Fully outfitted — refrigerator/freezer, gas stove with oven, dishwasher, disposal, microwave, coffee maker, electric kettle, toaster, toaster oven, blender, and full cookware/tableware.
  • Bathroom: Talavera-style bath with tub/shower.
  • Climate Control: Central heat and air conditioning; ceiling fans in living room, bedrooms and porch.
  • Washer & Dryer: In-unit laundry for longer stays.
  • Connectivity: Internet with Wi‑Fi and telephone for local/long-distance calls.
  • Safety: Basic security system on the property.
  • Outdoor Spaces: Screened porch, outdoor gas barbecue grill, campfire firepit with picnic table.
  • Water Activities: Kayak and canoe available with paddles and life vests.

Our cabin blends handcrafted rustic charm with conveniences to make your stay comfortable and memorable.
