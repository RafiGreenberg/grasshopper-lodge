About the Cabin

Grasshopper Lodge is a secluded, cozy, and romantic hand-crafted rustic cabin rental in the Ocala National Forest. Built from all-local wood, the cabin features cypress floors, knotty sand-pine walls and ceilings, and cedar doors and trim. It is the perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventurers alike.

The cabin sits on a corner of a 40-acre private parcel, surrounded on two sides by the Ocala National Forest. It is the ONLY house on an extensive chain of lakes that offer incredible recreation and wildlife viewing opportunities. Totally rustic, yet with all the amenities — fully equipped kitchen with microwave, dishwasher, disposal, washer and dryer, TV and home theater in living room and bedroom, ceiling fans, and screened porch.
