Our Story

Grasshopper Lodge is a secluded, cozy, and romantic hand-crafted rustic cabin rental in the Ocala National Forest. Built from all-local wood, the cabin features cypress floors, knotty sand-pine walls, and cedar doors. It is the perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventurers alike.
