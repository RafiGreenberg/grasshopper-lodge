Things to Do

Enjoy hiking, canoeing, fishing, and birdwatching in the Ocala National Forest. Sit on the dock and watch amazing sunsets, go kayaking on Big Grasshopper Lake, or explore the serene wilderness surrounding the lodge.

The Ocala National Forest offers incredible recreation and wildlife viewing opportunities. Only 50 miles from Orlando and 40 miles from Daytona Beach, use the lodge as your base to explore all that Central Florida has to offer, or come and recharge your batteries in the peaceful wilderness.
