Rates are based on occupancy by two persons. The only bed is a king-size bed in the master bedroom; additional guests such as small children may sleep on the living room sofa (which is not a pull-out), but the cabin is ideally designed for two.

There is a surcharge for additional guests of $50 per person per night. Taxes are not included. Minimum stay is 3 nights; occasionally longer minimum stays are required for holidays (for example, Thanksgiving or Christmas/New Year).
